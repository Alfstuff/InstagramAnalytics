Privacy policy

Last modified 21/11/2017

This web app is used to be able to look up public instagram accounts and to read the contents of an account. It is used to show statistics about a users account such as most liked pictures, most used filters, most used hashtags and things like that. This web application does nothing to modify accounts or its contents. It cannot post content or do any other user action for an Instagram account, it only READS, nothing else.
